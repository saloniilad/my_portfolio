My Journey

My journey into coding began in the second year of my engineering degree. What initially started as just part of my academic curriculum soon turned into something I genuinely enjoyed. The more I explored, the more I realized how much I love building things—from solving logic-based problems to creating something people can actually use.

As a Computer Engineering student, I’m steadily building a strong understanding of both hardware and software. I’ve always believed that technology should be meaningful and inclusive—so I aim to create projects that are not just technically sound, but also simple, accessible, and helpful to real users.

These days, I’m especially focused on web development and machine learning. I’ve been sharpening my frontend and backend skills, and I genuinely enjoy the learning process—whether it's through personal projects, college work, or hackathons. I find great energy in collaborating with like-minded peers and pushing myself to build solutions that have a real-world impact.
